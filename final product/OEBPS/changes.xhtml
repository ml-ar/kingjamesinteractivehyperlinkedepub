Editor’s Changes


The basis for the text is the standardized text of the King James Version or Authorized Version of the Holy Bible, using the standardized text of 1769. However, the extant transcriptions thereof on the Internet differ in a few places from one another, if not possessing outright and obvious errors. Here is the list of all changes I have manually made in an effort to bring it into accordance with the 1611 text (and if through an overworking of pride I have erred, I would welcome correction):

1 Esdras 9:49: changed errant period to comma, i.e., change "the chief priest. and reader" to ""the chief priest, and reader"

Tobit 3:10: change "Whe she" to "When she"

Judith 2:7: Change "declare unto that" into "declare unto them that"

Judith 6:9: Change "they shall be taken" into "they shall not be taken"

Judith 13:2: Change "And Judith was left along in the tent" to "And Judith was left alone in the tent"

Judith 11:10: Change "respect not" to "reject not"

Ecclesiaticus, A Prologue made by an uncertain Author: Changed "and called home a again" to "and called home again"

Ecclesiaticus 19:1: Remove the errant capital A, i.e., change "A labouring man that A is given" to "A labouring man that is given"

Ecclesiaticus 35:7: Change errant period to a comma, i.e., change "The sacrifice of a just man is acceptable. and" to "The sacrifice of a just man is acceptable, and"

Wisdom 4:4: Change "standing not last" to "standing not fast"

Wisdom 15:8: "and within a little while after returneth to the same, out when his life which was lent him shall be demanded." to "and within a little while after returneth to the same, out of the which he was taken, when his life which was lent him shall be demanded."

Wisdom 18:22: Change "with a word subdued him that punished" to "with a word subdued he him that punished"

Prayer of Manasseh 1:13: Fix obvious typo, i.e., change "mine iniquites" to "mine iniquities"

1 Macabees 9:50: Change "Beth-el" to "Bethel"

2 Maccabees 11:1: Change "not long after the" to "not long after this"
